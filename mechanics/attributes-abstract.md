# Attributes 

Abstract of the Attribute System of Phoenix  
*Flosha, July 20th, 2026*


## 1 
* Every NPC has (1) primary and (2) secondary attributes.
* Primary Attributes are divided in (1a) general attributes (all characters) and (1b) class-specific attributes (only characters of a specific class).
* Primary Attributes always have 10 maximum levels and are never displayed to the player as numerical values. Each level is represented by a descriptive term. 
* Secondary Attributes have differing maximum values and are displayed by Icons or Bars. 

## 2



* With a few exceptions monsters do not have class-specific attributes. Exceptions: (Orcish Shamans have Arcane Knowledge, the Sleeper and the Undead Shamans have Psionic Power).
